Our Story

LC Waikiki is more than just a clothing brand. We are a global fashion retailer committed to making fashion accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Quality, Affordability, and Style

Founded in France in 1988 and later acquired by Turkish company Taha Tekstil, LC Waikiki has grown into a global brand with a presence in dozens of countries. Our mission is simple: to offer fashionable and high-quality clothing at affordable prices, ensuring that everyone can express their style without breaking the bank.

We believe in customer happiness, quality without compromise, and continuous improvement. Explore our diverse collections that cater to all ages and styles, reflecting global trends with a local touch.
