Our Story

Founded in France in 1988 by Georges Amouyal and his partner, LC Waikiki initially started as a wholesale brand. In 1997, it was acquired by the Turkish company Taha Tekstil and transformed into a retail brand. Since then, LC Waikiki has grown exponentially, becoming a leading fashion retailer with thousands of stores across dozens of countries worldwide.

Our journey is defined by a commitment to quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction, expanding our global footprint while staying true to our roots.

Our Mission

To enable everyone to dress in accordance with their style and budget, offering a wide range of fashionable and high-quality clothing at affordable prices.

Our Vision

To be one of the top three leading fashion retail brands in Europe by 2023, while continuing to expand globally and enhance our customer experience.

Our Values

We believe in customer happiness, quality without compromise, teamwork, social responsibility, and continuous improvement in all aspects of our business.

Our Global Presence

With over 1200 stores in 56 countries, LC Waikiki continues to grow and bring affordable fashion to communities worldwide. Our diverse collections cater to all ages and styles, reflecting global trends with a local touch.
